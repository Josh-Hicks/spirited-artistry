About the Artist

I love expressing my artistic ideas with water colors. The medium itself releases a creative spirit in me, which is then reflected in my work. The special techniques I have learned to use with the medium help guide my paintings in the direction I want the depiction and mood to represent.

My interest in art began at the age of 12. My 5th grade teacher advised my parents that she felt I had artistic talent. I was awarded a scholarship to the Columbus College of Art & Design. By the age of 13 my work was displayed in the first annual "Arts Festival" in Columbus, Ohio.

Since that time I have experimented with many mediums, but I always come back to my old friend "water colors". Throughout the years I have won "Best of Show" in several art shows, and I have had the pleasure of sharing many paintings with many people. At this time I continue with my work, and I enjoy the freedom that it brings to my soul.

-Melissa
